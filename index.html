Simple Natural Recipes

Here will be some examples of very simple recipes that use plain ingredients. These recipes are good for simple home-cooking practice, and usually just require a little patience, time, or love.

Simple Butter Pie Crust

  1. Combine 1.25 Cups of All Purpose Flour with 0.25 Teaspoons Salt in a large bowl.
  2. Cut in 0.5 Cups chilled and diced Butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Stir in 0.25 Cups ice water one Tablespoon at a time, until the mixture forms a ball (you may need more or less water to form a ball).
  4. Wrap dough ball in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 4 hours, or as long as overnight.
  5. Remove refrigerated dough from plastic wrap and roll out (using floured surface and rolling pin) until it's large enough to fit a 9" pie plate, about 1/8" thick.
  6. Press dough evenly into the bottom and sides of the pie plate. Proceed with the rest of your desired recipe that uses unbaked pie shell.
